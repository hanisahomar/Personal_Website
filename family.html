Family background

I am the last child in my family and I also have an older brother. I live with my mom in our hometown in Pahang while my brother is currently living in Selangor. My mother is a single-mother. After my late father passed away in 2010, my mom has decided to move in into our hometown to take care about my late grandmother who has a kidney problem. So, my mother is the one who took care about my grandmother. My mom is a housewife and she has been unemployed since 2006. Our family is from Negeri Sembilan. Despite living in Pahang for a long time, we still talking in Negeri Sembilan's dialects every time we communicate each other.
